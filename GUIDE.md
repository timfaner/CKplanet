## Guide for CKPlanet ([link](https://github.com/timfaner/CKplanet/blob/master/GUIDE.md))
### Steps 
- Connect  wallet, keypering and ETH are both supported
- There will be ___two___ requires to sign . Confirm them.
![s3](https://github.com/timfaner/CKplanet/blob/master/screenshot4.png?raw=true)
![s4](https://github.com/timfaner/CKplanet/blob/master/screenshot3.png?raw=true)
- Type  a dataserver  to store your data. `https://ckplanet.beihanguni.cn` is available , or you can set [your own](https://github.com/timfaner/CKplanet/tree/master/file-server)
- Confirm the transaction, it will take about 1~2 min.
- Select nickname, avatar and create profile.
- All set
- Your can join a guide Planet after login  to know more. In search filed just type `0x7be7a7f5a71916338778348531012a82c658e057` in lock_args and `wvOH` in planet id. Then you will get a page like ![s1](https://github.com/timfaner/CKplanet/blob/master/screenshot2.png?raw=true)
